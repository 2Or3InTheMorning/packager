TurboWarp Packager Privacy Policy

Updated June 22nd, 2021

The website (packager.turbowarp.org)

We may log anonymous interaction data such as how many people are using the site at a given time, how many times a button is pressed, what operating system and browser is being used, and a user's location to the nearest country. We do not log IP addresses. We do not serve ads. We do not track our users.

You can opt-out by enable your browser's "Do Not Track" setting.

The site may contact Scratch to download projects. See the Scratch privacy policy for more information.

The output of the website ("packaged projects")

If a packaged project contains cloud variables and it is configured to "Connect to cloud variable server", then the packaged project may connect to a TurboWarp server. See the primary TurboWarp privacy policy for more information. Aside from that, packaged projects do not connect to external servers unless the packaged project has been manually modified (for example, using the custom JS feature).

Contact

Any concerns related to privacy or any other matter should be sent to: contact@turbowarp.org
